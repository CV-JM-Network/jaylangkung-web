Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our networking services and solutions

How long does a typical network implementation take?

Implementation timelines vary based on project scope and complexity. Small office networks typically take 2-4 weeks, while enterprise deployments can range from 2-6 months. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase and work closely with your team to minimize business disruption.

Do you provide 24/7 support for all networking solutions?

We offer tiered support options to match your needs and budget. Our Premium and Enterprise support tiers include 24/7 monitoring and support. Basic support provides business hours coverage with emergency escalation procedures. All tiers include remote monitoring and proactive issue detection.

Can you work with our existing network infrastructure?

Absolutely! We specialize in both greenfield deployments and brownfield upgrades. Our team will assess your current infrastructure and design solutions that maximize your existing investments while addressing performance, security, and scalability requirements.

What certifications and compliance standards do you support?

Our team holds industry certifications including CCIE, CISSP, and vendor-specific credentials. We design networks to meet various compliance requirements including HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOX, FISMA, and ISO 27001. Each solution is tailored to your specific regulatory and security requirements.

Do you provide network monitoring and management services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive network monitoring and management services. This includes real-time performance monitoring, proactive issue detection, automated alerting, performance reporting, and remote troubleshooting. Our NOC operates 24/7 to ensure optimal network performance.

How do you ensure network security and data protection?

Security is integrated into every aspect of our network designs. We implement defense-in-depth strategies including network segmentation, access controls, encryption, intrusion detection, and regular security assessments. All solutions follow industry best practices and compliance requirements.
