Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Brainet internet service

What makes fiber internet better than cable or DSL?

Fiber-optic internet uses light signals through glass fibers, providing much faster speeds, lower latency, and more reliable connections than copper-based technologies. Unlike cable, fiber doesn't slow down during peak usage times, and unlike DSL, performance doesn't degrade with distance.

How long does installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within 2-4 hours. The exact time depends on your home's layout and whether new fiber needs to be run to your location. We offer same-day installation for many areas, and our technicians will provide an accurate time estimate during scheduling.

Are there any data caps or throttling?

No, all Brainet plans include unlimited data with no caps, throttling, or restrictions. You get the full speed you pay for, 24/7, whether you're streaming, gaming, or running a business.

What equipment is included with my plan?

All residential plans include a high-performance WiFi router at no additional cost. Business plans include enterprise-grade equipment appropriate for your needs. We also provide the fiber modem (ONT) and all necessary cabling.

What's your uptime guarantee?

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for residential customers and up to 99.99% for enterprise customers. If we don't meet our SLA, you'll receive service credits. Our network includes redundant fiber paths and backup systems to minimize any potential downtime.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time with no fees. Speed upgrades are often immediate, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. Our customer portal makes it easy to manage your account and make changes online.
